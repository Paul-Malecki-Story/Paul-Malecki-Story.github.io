About Paul

Paul Malecki, a devoted husband, father, and friend, is bravely battling stage 4 lung cancer. Despite the challenges, Paul's spirit remains strong. He has touched many lives with his warmth and humor. We’ve created this space to share updates on his journey and offer ways to support him and his family.
