About Paul

Paul Malecki is a 54-year-old father, husband, and friend to many who was diagnosed with stage 4 lung cancer in early 2022.

Despite the challenges of his diagnosis, Paul has remained an inspiration to all who know him with his positive attitude, sense of humor, and determination to fight.

Before his diagnosis, Paul was an active member of his community, coaching youth sports and volunteering at his church. He worked as an electrician for over 25 years and takes great pride in his work.

Paul's Family

Paul is married to his wife Sarah of 28 years, and they have two children - Michael (25) and Emily (22). The Malecki family appreciates all the love and support they've received during this difficult time.
